Asia Music & Performing Arts Education (AMPA Education) is the only distributor of international music & performing arts curricula and certifications in the Southeast Asia region. With our significant experience music and the performing arts education, we are able to offer strong and unrivaled insight into the music & performing arts education industry in the region. We therefore have the ability to boost the development of these products in this growing market, by matching product features to specific customer needs, creating demand for these products and providing them to the best fit target segments.

As a pioneer in this developing industry, we have built strong relationships with international music and performing arts educational institutions and publishers. These include Trinity College London, Imperial Society of Teachers of Dancing (ISTD), National Association of Teachers of Dancing (NATD), Kindermusik and Alfred Music.
